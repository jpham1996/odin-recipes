Banh Mi Ingredients

The special kind of sandwich

Hybrid combination of French and Vietnamese cuisine

A Vietnamese street food sandwich that originated in Saigon.

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 small cucumber or 1/2 large cucumber
  • 1/2 cup of cilantro
  • 2 green chili peppers
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/8 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 12 ounces boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1.5 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 (13-inch) baguette
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Maggi Seasoning

Recipe Instructions

  1. Mince the garlic.
  2. Peel the carrot and chop it into matchstick pieces. Read More: https://www.tastingtable.com/649760/simple-banh-mi-sandwich-recipe/
  3. Peel and slice the cucumber into ribbons.
  4. Pick the cilantro leaves off the stems.
  5. Slice the green chilies.
  6. Mix the sugar, rice wine vinegar, and salt with the hot water until the sugar and salt have dissolved. Read More: https://www.tastingtable.com/649760/simple-banh-mi-sandwich-recipe/
  7. Pour the mixture over the carrots and let sit to pickle for 1 hour. Read More: https://www.tastingtable.com/649760/simple-banh-mi-sandwich-recipe/
  8. Chop the chicken thigh meat into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large bowl. Read More: https://www.tastingtable.com/649760/simple-banh-mi-sandwich-recipe/
  9. Mix the minced garlic, sesame oil, honey, hoisin sauce, and soy sauce, and pour it over the chicken. Read More: https://www.tastingtable.com/649760/simple-banh-mi-sandwich-recipe/
  10. Refrigerate the chicken for 15 minutes.
  11. Heat a frying pan over medium-high and sauté the marinated chicken until cooked through, about 10 minutes Read More: https://www.tastingtable.com/649760/simple-banh-mi-sandwich-recipe/
  12. Drain the pickled carrots.
  13. Slice open the baguette and scoop out some of the soft bread inside to create more room for the sandwich fillings. Read More: https://www.tastingtable.com/649760/simple-banh-mi-sandwich-recipe/
  14. Spread mayonnaise onto both sides of the baguette, then sprinkle it with Maggi Seasoning. Read More: https://www.tastingtable.com/649760/simple-banh-mi-sandwich-recipe/
  15. Place the cooked chicken inside the bread followed by carrots, cucumber, cilantro, and chili peppers. Read More: https://www.tastingtable.com/649760/simple-banh-mi-sandwich-recipe/
  16. Slice the bánh mì in half to make two servings and enjoy! Read More: https://www.tastingtable.com/649760/simple-banh-mi-sandwich-recipe/
